About

I’m Sepideh Lotfollahi, a Tehran‑based fashion designer and graphic artist. My background is in graphic design (B.A., University of Mazandaran). I blend pattern‑making and garment construction with visual storytelling—from textbook character illustration to product UI. Tools I use every day include Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, After Effects and XD.

I’m available for collaborations on apparel design and sampling, illustration for education, and UI design for consumer apps.
